# IntelGraph — Vision

## One-Line Vision

Transform raw threat intelligence into structured, explainable, and actionable knowledge graphs.

## Strategic Vision

IntelGraph exists to solve the fundamental challenge of threat intelligence: **IOCs without context are noise, not intelligence.**

Modern SOC teams are flooded with threat feeds. The problem isn't access to data — it's knowing which indicators to trust, how they relate to each other, and why a particular alert matters. IntelGraph bridges this gap by:

- **Cross-validating indicators** across multiple CTI sources before flagging them as malicious
- **Building evidence chains** that trace every conclusion back to its source
- **Organizing threats into a knowledge graph** where analysts can pivot, investigate, and export findings

## Core Beliefs

1. Threat intelligence without evidence is speculation
2. Correlation without explainability creates false confidence
3. Intelligence sharing without standards (STIX 2.1) creates silos
4. SOC tools must integrate — not replace — existing workflows

## What IntelGraph Is

- An open-source threat intelligence correlation engine
- A REST API-first platform for SOC enrichment and investigation
- A knowledge graph builder with evidence-backed reasoning
- A STIX 2.1-native export and sharing tool

## What IntelGraph Is NOT

- Not a replacement for OpenCTI or MISP — it complements them
- Not a SIEM or real-time log monitor
- Not a private data broker or surveillance platform
- Not a malware sandbox or file analysis tool

## Target Users

- SOC analysts and incident responders
- Threat intelligence researchers
- CTI platform operators (MISP, OpenCTI)
- Security engineers building enrichment pipelines

## Five-Year Horizon

IntelGraph should become the go-to open-source enrichment engine for SOC teams — trusted for its explainability, integrated via its REST API, and deployed alongside existing SIEM and TIP workflows.
